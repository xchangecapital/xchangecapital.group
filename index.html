PLACE IN THE MARKET

The global network sets the trend in meeting the needs of the market for goods and services. Increasingly, people are looking for product information on the Internet, click on advertising banners on websites, use content from the pages of well-known online brands on social networks, and recommend web resources to their friends.

HIGH COMPETITION

The toughening of competition in Internet commerce is largely due to the increase in the number of mobile users. People go online via smartphones 3 times more often than other devices. And more than 50 percent of users look for product information through their phones before buying.

USER FACTORS

At the same time, the appearance of the site, the ability to subscribe to social networks, the quality of content and adaptability to mobile platforms play an important role for visitors. So, 75% of users leave the service or product page if it takes a long time to load or is not optimized for their device.
